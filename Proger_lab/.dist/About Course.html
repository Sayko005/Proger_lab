Frequently Asked Questions

What is Proger Lab?

Proger Lab is a specialized preparatory course designed to help students succeed in the Unified National Testing (UNT) in Kazakhstan. We offer tailored study plans, expert instructors, and comprehensive resources to boost your performance in all core subjects.

How long is the Proger Lab course?

The Proger Lab course typically runs for 6-9 months, depending on the student’s individual needs. We also offer shorter, intensive courses closer to the UNT exam date for last-minute preparation.

How does Proger Lab help improve UNT scores?

We use a results-driven approach that includes diagnostic testing, personalized study plans, practice exams, and regular feedback to ensure consistent progress. Our methods are focused on building subject knowledge and test-taking strategies.

Is Proger Lab available online or in-person?

We offer both online and in-person classes. Our online platform provides interactive lessons, video tutorials, and practice tests, ensuring the same high-quality experience as in-person learning.

How do I enroll in Proger Lab?

You can enroll by visiting our website or contacting us directly via phone or email. Our team will guide you through the registration process and help you select the course that best fits your needs.
